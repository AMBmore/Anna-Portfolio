# VSO Study Guide: The Spirit Horse Returns

This study guide was developed for distribution across school divisions in British Columbia, targeting students from kindergarten to grade seven as part of Symphonic Encounters, a field trip program designed to engage children with orchestral music. Created in Canva, the guide supports The Spirit Horse Returns, an orchestral work exploring the history and legends of the near-extinct Ojibwe horses. Visual assets include images used with permission from Indigenous artist Rhonda Snow, originally featured in the production, as well as AI-generated imagery created using Canva’s Magic Media™ text-to-image tool.

I was tasked with researching the original production, performing editorial revisions to the script, and designing the guide layout. Additional responsibilities involved embedding video content, integrating clickable hyperlinks, and developing UI/UX-focused content for accessibility and engagement. The final study guide adheres to Vancouver Symphony Orchestra UX and branding standards.
