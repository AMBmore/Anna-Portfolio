# Anna's Bio

My background in technical writing, UX content strategy, and instructional design allow me to translate complex information into intuitive, user-centered experiences. I’ve developed UX content and design solutions that increased user engagement by 20–25%, and produced accessible, style-guide aligned digital assets such as learning guides, newsletters, and web content. Additionally, I have produced 25-page technical documents under two-week deadlines by collaborating closely with engineers and cross-functional teams, demonstrating my ability to communicate design approaches, clarify requirements, and coordinate workflows between technical teams and stakeholders.

Across roles, I’ve consistently applied essential design principles to improve clarity, usability, and audience experience. My work in education strengthened my ability to understand user behavior: I improved learning outcomes for 30-student classes at UBC by designing instructional materials, leading tutorials, and providing targeted feedback, and I mentored over 150 students through multi-day interactive workshops built around evidence-based learning strategies. 

Beyond my UX and instructional design work, I bring a strong creative foundation as a writer, editor, and event curator with published work across multiple platforms and a WordPress blog that showcases my writing, reviews, and creative projects. This interdisciplinary practice helps me approach design problems with both analytical and narrative awareness, understanding not only how users interact with interfaces, but how they connect emotionally with stories, communities, and shared experiences.
