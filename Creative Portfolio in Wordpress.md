# Creative Portfolio in WordPress

View my Creative Portolio here: 

[Untitiled Unmastered - Home](https://amblackmore.wordpress.com/)
[Untitiled Unmastered - Blog](https://amblackmore.wordpress.com/blog/)

My WordPress site Untitled Unmastered was developed in 2019 as a centralized platform to document and present a range of creative work. The homepage functions as a professional landing page used for applications (e.g. grants and submissions), while the blog serves as a structured, scrollable archive of projects, written pieces, and multimedia content.

Designing and maintaining the site provided hands-on experience in UX/UI principles and front-end troubleshooting, including content organization and navigation design. The site is continuously updated to reflect ongoing work and evolving projects.

## Key content areas include:
- Performance documentation and media, including embedded video and audio.
- Long-form written content (essays, reviews, and personal narratives)
- Creative writing (poetry)
- Visual design work
- Publication and presentation history
- Professional biography and academic background

The site is designed for a general audience, including peers, collaborators, and individuals interested in interdisciplinary artistry. To maintain a controlled and distraction-free user experience, interactive features such as comments and “likes” were intentionally excluded, positioning the site as a curated archive rather than a social platform.

## Tools and platforms used:
- Microsoft Office
- Canva
- YouTube
- SoundCloud
- Splice - Video Editor and Maker iOS app. 

Users may subscribe to my WordPress blog to receive new content direcetly to their email inbox.
